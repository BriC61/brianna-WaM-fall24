ABOUT BLOOMCAST

BLOOMCAST uses the flower of the month as an indicator of local weather. by inputting one's zip code, the flower will depict the local conditions: humidity will determine the number of flowers, wind speed will determine the angle of the flowers, and temperature will determine the height.additionally, the background reflects the time of day and any precipitation conditions (snow, rain, clouds, etc)

further down the page is additional information about the flower, such as its meaning in different regions and recipes that incorporate the flower. the meaning is swapped out every day, so there is always new information about the flower on return visits.
